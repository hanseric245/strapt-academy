Meet Your Instructor

Learn from one of the best in the industry

Buck Lawler

Head Instructor / USPSA Grand Master

"I'm Buck Lawler, USPSA Grand Master and lead instructor at Strapt Academy. I have devoted thousands of hours to reach that level — but more importantly, I've helped countless shooters break through their plateaus and finally see real improvement."

I've trained diverse audiences including new gun owners, competition shooters, SWAT teams, and military personnel. Every shooter is different, but the fundamentals remain the same. My approach focuses on practical, real-world skill development rather than gear collection.

I earned USPSA Grandmaster using my everyday carry — the same gun I rely on to protect my family. I'm one of only two people to achieve USPSA Grandmaster status exclusively through Appendix Inside the Waistband (AIWB) carry.
